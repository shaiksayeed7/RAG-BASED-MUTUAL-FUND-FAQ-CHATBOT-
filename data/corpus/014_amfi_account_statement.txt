AMFI - HOW TO DOWNLOAD MUTUAL FUND ACCOUNT STATEMENT (CAS)

What is a CAS (Consolidated Account Statement)?
A CAS (Consolidated Account Statement) is a single statement that consolidates details of all your mutual fund investments across all AMCs linked to your PAN. It is issued by NSDL and CDSL (Registrars/Depositories) and sent to investors' registered email addresses monthly if there are any transactions.

How to Download CAS (Consolidated Account Statement)?

Method 1: Through CAMS (Computer Age Management Services)
Website: https://www.camsonline.com/InvestorServices/CAMSstatement
1. Go to https://www.camsonline.com
2. Click on "Investor Services" → "CAS - CAMS + KFintech"
3. Enter your registered email ID or PAN
4. Select the statement type and period
5. Enter your PAN and date of birth/folio number for authentication
6. Statement will be sent to your registered email address

Method 2: Through KFintech (formerly Karvy)
Website: https://www.kfintech.com/investor-services
1. Go to https://mfs.kfintech.com
2. Click on "Account Statement"
3. Enter PAN, email, and other details
4. Statement delivered to email

Method 3: Through NSDL (For Demat account holders)
Website: https://eservices.nsdl.com

Method 4: Through AMC Website (Scheme-specific statement)
HDFC Mutual Fund: https://www.hdfcfund.com/service/download-account-statement
1. Log in to HDFC Fund online portal with PAN/folio number
2. Go to "Account Statement"
3. Select date range and download

Method 5: Through Groww App
1. Open Groww app
2. Go to Profile → Reports
3. Select "Account Statement"
4. Download for required period

How to Download Capital Gains Statement?
A Capital Gains Statement shows your realized gains/losses from mutual fund redemptions during a financial year. Used for income tax filing.

From CAMS: https://www.camsonline.com/InvestorServices/CAMSstatement
1. Visit https://www.camsonline.com
2. Select "Capital Gains Statement" option
3. Enter PAN, registered email, financial year
4. Statement sent to email

From HDFC Fund Website: https://www.hdfcfund.com/service/tax-documents
- Login to HDFC Fund online portal
- Go to "Tax Documents" → "Capital Gains Statement"
- Select financial year and download

How to Download ELSS Tax Statement?
For Section 80C tax-saving proof of ELSS investment:
- From HDFC Fund: https://www.hdfcfund.com/service/tax-documents
- From CAMS: https://www.camsonline.com (Investor Services → Tax Statements)
- The statement shows investment amounts eligible for 80C deduction

Statement Formats: Statements are available in PDF format (encrypted with PAN and DOB) and Excel format.
