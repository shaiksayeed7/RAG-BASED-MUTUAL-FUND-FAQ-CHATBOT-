What is a Flexi Cap Fund?
A Flexi Cap Fund is an open-ended dynamic equity scheme that can invest across market capitalizations — large cap, mid cap, and small cap companies — with no fixed allocation constraint. As per SEBI categorization, at least 65% of assets must be in equity and equity-related instruments.
