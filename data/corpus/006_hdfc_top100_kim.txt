HDFC TOP 100 FUND - KEY INFORMATION MEMORANDUM (KIM)

Scheme Name: HDFC Top 100 Fund
Type: Open-Ended Equity Scheme predominantly investing in Large Cap Stocks
Category: Large Cap Fund

Fundamental Attributes:
- Investment Universe: Large Cap companies (top 100 stocks by market cap as defined by AMFI)
- Fund Focus: Capital appreciation through equity and equity related securities of large cap companies

Load Structure:
Entry Load: Not Applicable (SEBI has abolished entry load since August 2009)
Exit Load:
  - 1% on redemption/switch-out of units within 365 days (1 year) from date of allotment
  - Nil on redemption/switch-out of units after 365 days from date of allotment
  - Exit load is not applicable on STP (Systematic Transfer Plan) registered before October 22, 2022

Recurring Expenses (Expense Ratio):
- Regular Plan: Up to 2.25% per annum (actual ~1.76% as of November 2024)
- Direct Plan: Lower than Regular Plan (actual ~0.87% as of November 2024)
- The total expense ratio (TER) is disclosed monthly by the AMC on AMFI website and its own website

Minimum Application Amount:
- Fresh Purchase: Rs. 100 and any amount thereafter
- Additional Purchase: Rs. 100 and any amount thereafter
- SIP: Rs. 100 per installment (minimum 6 installments)

Benchmark: NIFTY 100 TRI
Riskometer Level: VERY HIGH
(This product is suitable for investors who are seeking long-term capital growth and who understand that their principal will be at very high risk)

Dividend/IDCW Policy:
- IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) is paid at the discretion of the fund manager
- IDCW is not guaranteed
- Upon payment of IDCW, the NAV of the scheme will fall to the extent of the IDCW paid

Tax Benefits: None (Not an ELSS scheme)

Section 80C: NOT APPLICABLE (HDFC Top 100 Fund does not qualify for Section 80C deduction)

Investor Suitability:
The fund is suitable for investors who:
1. Seek long-term capital appreciation
2. Have an investment horizon of 3 years or more
3. Are comfortable with very high risk to their principal

Important Note: Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks. Read all scheme-related documents carefully before investing.
