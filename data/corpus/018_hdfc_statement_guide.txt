HDFC MUTUAL FUND - HOW TO DOWNLOAD ACCOUNT STATEMENT

HDFC Mutual Fund offers multiple ways to download your account statement.

Method 1: HDFC Fund Online Portal
Website: https://www.hdfcfund.com/service/download-account-statement
Steps:
1. Visit https://www.hdfcfund.com
2. Click on "Login" (top right corner)
3. Enter your User ID (PAN or Folio Number) and password
4. Go to "Transact" → "Account Statement"
5. Select statement type: Regular Statement or Capital Gains Statement
6. Choose start date and end date
7. Click "Download" - PDF will be generated and sent to your registered email

Method 2: Without Login (Email-based)
1. Visit https://www.hdfcfund.com/service/download-account-statement
2. Enter your email address and PAN
3. Select statement period
4. Statement is emailed to your registered email address

Method 3: WhatsApp
1. Save HDFC Fund's WhatsApp number: +91-22-6751-6400
2. Send "Hi" to start
3. Select "Account Statement" option
4. Follow the prompts to receive your statement

Method 4: IVR (Phone)
Call HDFC Mutual Fund: 1800-3010-6767 (toll-free) or 044-49099000
Request account statement through IVR

Statement Types Available:
1. Transaction Statement: Shows all your transactions (purchases, redemptions, dividends, SIP installments)
2. Capital Gains Statement: Shows realized capital gains/losses for income tax filing
3. Portfolio Holdings Statement: Current holdings with purchase details
4. ELSS Tax Statement: Investment details for Section 80C deduction (for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver)

How to Download Capital Gains Statement from HDFC Fund:
1. Login to https://www.hdfcfund.com
2. Go to "Tax Reports" → "Capital Gains Statement"
3. Select the financial year (e.g., April 2023 to March 2024)
4. Download PDF or Excel format

Statement from CAMS (for all AMCs including HDFC):
CAMS manages HDFC Mutual Fund registrar services.
Website: https://www.camsonline.com/InvestorServices/CAMSstatement
1. Visit CAMS website
2. Select "CAS" (Consolidated Account Statement) - covers all CAMS-managed AMCs
3. Enter email ID and PAN
4. Receive statement at registered email

Password for PDF Statement:
HDFC Fund statements are typically encrypted with the first 8 characters of PAN (uppercase) + date of birth in DDMMYYYY format.
Example: If PAN is ABCDE1234F and DOB is 01/01/1990, password = ABCDE1230 1011990

Note: Do not share your account statement password with anyone. HDFC Mutual Fund will never ask for your password.
