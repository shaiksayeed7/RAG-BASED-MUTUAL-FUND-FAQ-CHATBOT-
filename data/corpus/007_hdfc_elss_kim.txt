HDFC ELSS TAX SAVER - KEY INFORMATION MEMORANDUM (KIM)

Scheme Name: HDFC ELSS Tax Saver
Type: Open-Ended Equity Linked Saving Scheme with a statutory lock-in of 3 years and tax benefit
Category: ELSS

Statutory Lock-in:
- Mandatory lock-in period: 3 years from the date of allotment
- Units cannot be redeemed, pledged, or transferred before completion of 3 years
- For SIP investments, each installment has its own 3-year lock-in from its allotment date
- Example: SIP installment paid on January 15, 2022 matures on January 15, 2025

Tax Deduction under Section 80C:
- Investments qualify for deduction up to Rs. 1,50,000 per financial year under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961
- Tax benefit applicable only under the OLD tax regime
- Under new tax regime (default from FY 2024-25), Section 80C deductions are NOT available
- ELSS is one of the eligible instruments under Section 80C (along with PPF, NSC, life insurance, etc.)

Load Structure:
Entry Load: Not Applicable
Exit Load: NIL
(Since all units are locked in for 3 years, no exit load is levied after the lock-in period ends.)

Recurring Expenses (Expense Ratio):
- Regular Plan: Up to 2.25% per annum (actual ~1.73% as of November 2024)
- Direct Plan: Lower than Regular Plan (actual ~1.02% as of November 2024)

Minimum Application Amount:
- Fresh Purchase (Lump Sum): Rs. 500 and any amount thereafter
- Additional Purchase: Rs. 500 and any amount thereafter
- SIP: Rs. 500 per installment (minimum 3 installments)

Benchmark: NIFTY 500 TRI
Riskometer Level: VERY HIGH

Long-Term Capital Gains Tax (Post Lock-in):
- After the 3-year lock-in, if units are held for more than 1 year (which they will be since lock-in is 3 years), they qualify for Long-Term Capital Gains
- LTCG tax: 12.5% on gains exceeding Rs. 1,25,000 per year (as per Finance Act 2024)
- No indexation benefit for equity mutual funds

How ELSS Saves Tax:
A taxpayer in the 30% tax bracket investing Rs. 1,50,000 in ELSS saves up to Rs. 46,800 in taxes (Rs. 1,50,000 × 30% + 4% cess) under the old tax regime.

Note: ELSS does NOT guarantee returns. The tax saving is on the investment amount, not on returns.
