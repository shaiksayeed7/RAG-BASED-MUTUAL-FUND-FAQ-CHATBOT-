## Factual Questions (Answered)

### Q1: What is the expense ratio of HDFC Top 100 Fund?

**A:** The expense ratio of HDFC Top 100 Fund is **1.76% per annum** for the Regular Plan and **0.87% per annum** for the Direct Plan (as of November 2024). The expense ratio is the annual fee deducted from the fund's assets for managing the scheme.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-top-100-fund  
**Also see:** https://www.amfiindia.com/expense-ratio

---

### Q2: What is the lock-in period for ELSS funds?

**A:** The lock-in period for ELSS (Equity Linked Saving Scheme) funds is **3 years (36 months)** from the date of allotment. This is a statutory requirement under the Equity Linked Saving Scheme, 2005 notification by the Ministry of Finance. For SIP investments, each installment has its own 3-year lock-in from its respective allotment date.

**Source:** https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/knowledge-center/elss.html  
**Also see:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-elss-tax-saver

---

### Q3: What is the minimum SIP amount for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver?

**A:** The minimum SIP amount for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver is **Rs. 500 per installment** with a minimum of 3 installments. For lump sum investment, the minimum is also Rs. 500.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-elss-tax-saver  
**Also see:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/content/dam/abc/india/assets/images/kim/hdfc-elss-tax-saver-kim.pdf

### Q4: What is the exit load for HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?

**A:** The exit load for HDFC Flexi Cap Fund is **1% if redeemed or switched-out within 1 year (365 days)** from the date of allotment. There is no exit load after 1 year.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-flexicap-fund  
**Also see:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/loads

---

### Q5: What is the benchmark index for HDFC Top 100 Fund?

**A:** The benchmark index for HDFC Top 100 Fund is **NIFTY 100 TRI (Total Return Index)**. The NIFTY 100 tracks the performance of the top 100 companies by full market capitalization on the NSE. SEBI mandates the use of TRI (Total Return Index) as benchmark for all mutual fund schemes.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-top-100-fund  
**Also see:** https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/knowledge-center/benchmark.html

---

### Q6: What is the riskometer level of HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund?

**A:** The riskometer level of HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund is **Very High Risk**, meaning the principal is at very high risk due to equity market volatility. This is standard for equity mutual funds in India as per SEBI's riskometer guidelines.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-midcap-opportunities-fund  
**Also see:** https://www.sebi.gov.in/legal/circulars/oct-2020/product-labeling-in-mutual-funds-existing-scheme-riskometer.html

### Q7: How do I download my capital gains statement from HDFC Mutual Fund?

**A:** You can download your capital gains statement from HDFC Mutual Fund by logging in to https://www.hdfcfund.com, going to "Tax Reports" → "Capital Gains Statement", and selecting the financial year. Alternatively, visit https://www.camsonline.com and select "Capital Gains Statement", enter your PAN and registered email, and the statement will be sent to your email.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/service/tax-documents  
**Also see:** https://www.camsonline.com/InvestorServices/CAMSstatement

---

### Q8: Does HDFC ELSS Tax Saver qualify for Section 80C tax deduction?

**A:** Yes, investments in HDFC ELSS Tax Saver qualify for tax deduction under **Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961**, up to **Rs. 1,50,000 per financial year**. This tax benefit is available only under the old tax regime; under the new tax regime (default from FY 2024-25), Section 80C deductions are not available.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/content/dam/abc/india/assets/images/kim/hdfc-elss-tax-saver-kim.pdf  
**Also see:** https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/knowledge-center/elss.html

---

### Q9: What is the minimum SIP amount for HDFC Top 100 Fund and HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?

**A:** The minimum SIP amount for both **HDFC Top 100 Fund** and **HDFC Flexi Cap Fund** is **Rs. 100 per installment** with a minimum of 6 installments. The minimum lump sum investment is also Rs. 100 for these schemes.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-top-100-fund  
**Also see:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-flexicap-fund

---

### Q10: What is the exit load for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver?

**A:** The exit load for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver is **Nil**. Since all units in ELSS are subject to a mandatory 3-year statutory lock-in period, no exit load is charged after the lock-in period ends.

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equity-funds/hdfc-elss-tax-saver  
**Also see:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/loads
